About

A practical advisory built around trust, structure, and responsibility.

Amanah Business Advisory exists to help businesses become more organised, credible, and ready for larger opportunities.

Founder

Founded by Abbas Mohamed.

Amanah Business Advisory was founded by Abbas Mohamed, a Business & Economics student with experience across logistics, sales, retail, customer-facing work, and small business management.

The advisory is built around a practical understanding of how businesses operate day to day: coordinating people, managing suppliers, improving workflows, communicating with stakeholders, and turning ideas into structured action.

The focus is especially suited to small businesses, project-based companies, and early-stage ventures that need professional support without the cost or complexity of a large consultancy.

Values

What Amanah means in practice.

Trust

Work should be handled with honesty, confidentiality, and respect for the client’s goals and situation.

Clarity

Good business requires clear documents, clear responsibilities, clear pricing, and clear communication.

Practical execution

The goal is not theory for its own sake, but useful systems, documents, and support that help work move forward.
