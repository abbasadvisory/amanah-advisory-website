Privacy Policy

How we handle information submitted through this website.

This privacy policy explains how Amanah Consulting handles basic personal information submitted through the contact form or direct communication.

Information we collect

When you contact Amanah Consulting through the website, we may collect your name, email address, company or organisation name, and the message or project description you provide.

How we use the information

The information is used only to respond to your enquiry, understand your request, and communicate with you about possible consulting support.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may only be shared if necessary to respond to your enquiry, comply with legal obligations, or with your permission.

Data retention

Enquiry information is kept only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and manage communication.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have submitted by contacting us.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact Amanah Consulting through the contact page.
